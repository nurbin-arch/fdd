Event Details

Dashain Fest Day

📅 September 5, 2025 🕙 9:00 AM 📍 Techspire College

Dashain Fest is a celebration of culture, togetherness, and joy. As one of the most cherished festivals, it brings our community closer through music, food, and shared traditions. At Techspire Events, we combine the spirit of Dashain with inclusivity and fun, creating a space where people from all backgrounds can come together to celebrate heritage, strengthen bonds, and enjoy moments of happiness and unity.

International Food Day

📅 October 12, 2025 🕚 11:00 AM 📍 Techspire College Courtyard

A community potluck and tasting event featuring dishes from students' home countries. Includes recipe cards, cooking demos, and stories behind the meals to foster cultural exchange.

Cultural Dance Night

📅 November 8, 2025 🕡 6:30 PM 📍 Techspire Auditorium

An evening of traditional and contemporary dance performances celebrating cultures across our international student community. Open floor sessions and attire showcase included.

Hackathon 3.0

📅 22 September, 2025 🕖 7:00 AM 📍 Techspire College

Our Hackathons are all about innovation, collaboration, and creativity. We bring together developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and problem-solvers to brainstorm, build, and launch ideas that can make a real impact. With mentorship, teamwork, and cutting-edge challenges, the hackathon is not just a competition it's an opportunity to learn, grow, and be part of a community that thrives on turning bold ideas into reality.

Techspire Got Talent

📅 22 November, 2025 🕖 10:00 AM 📍 Techspire College

TechSpire Got Talent is a celebration of creativity, skills, and self-expression. This vibrant event gives individuals a platform to showcase their unique talents from music, dance, and art to innovation, storytelling, and beyond. More than just a talent show, it’s an opportunity to inspire, appreciate diversity, and strengthen community bonds through shared passion and performance.

Christmas Carol

📅 30 December, 2025 🕑 10:00 AM 📍 Techspire College

Our Christmas Carol celebration is filled with joy, music, and holiday spirit. This festive gathering brings people together to share melodies of hope and togetherness during the Christmas season. With carols, performances, and a cheerful atmosphere, we create a warm space where everyone can experience the magic of Christmas and the joy of giving.
